Welcome to Rugger Machine And Projects LTD

WHO ARE WE?

We are duly registered company by corprate affiars commission, operation in Nigeria, Rugger Machine And Projects LTD is turly recognoized as a solution provider with an extensive porfolio of four divisions; build sales and services of Power generators, heavy equipments, electrical panel controls and renewable energy Solutions, outsourcing.

Rugged Machines And Projects LTD was established with the objective of carrying out quality and dependable Services. Its business includes build, sales, servcing, mainatenance, overhauling and rentals of heavy equipments, Diesel and gas power generating sets. Since its establishment, it has been contributing to the development of power and construction sector in Nigeria economy, and became a one-stop-shop for Power Solution.
